Rakan St John

Empowering the community with the help of community

Rakan St. John is established to empower the public who would like to learn basic First Aid skills and volunteer themselves for charity. Introduction classes to Basic First Aid are organised to equip the public with basic first aid knowledge and skills. Charity and volunteering activities are also being held. Join us today!
