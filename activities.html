Blood Donation

Since 2007, Kawasan Selangor Tengah Utara (KSTU) consistency in holding quarterly blood donations with the National Blood Bank, with each year the collection of blood increase. Todate, KSTU collected more than 10,000 pints of blood and hopefully this feat will continue for the benefit of the people in need and more lives can be saved. For their effort, Kawasan Selangor Tengah Utara was awarded numerous Certificate of Appreciation & Certificate of Acknowledgement.
